How you would check the engine has sufficient oil on this vehicle.

Oil Check

    1. Volvo B7 RLE & Scania (single decker) - dipstick
    2. Open engine hood and remove the dipstick
    3. Wipe dipstick clean and re insert
    4. Remove and check the level is between minimum and maximum
    5. Other buses: start engine, read warning lights, seek engineer.
