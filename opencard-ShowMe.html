How you would check that all doors including cargo doors are secure.

Securing doors

    1.Walkaround and check
    2. Inspection panels
    3. Battery panel
    4. Engine panels front and rear
    5. Oil/AdBlue panels

SHOW ME


How you would check for AIR leaks on this vehicle.

Air Leak

    1. Handbrake
    2. Turn on Master switch and ignition
    3. Switch on ignition
    4. Dashboard Guages 8bar/120psi/ greenzone
    5. Walkaround & Listen & Report

SHOW ME


How you would check the correct AIR pressure on this vehicle.

Air Pressure

    1. Handbrake & Neutral
    2. Master Switch & Ignition
    3. Dashboard lights off
    4. Buzzers not sounding
    5. Guages Read 8bar/120psi/ greenzone

SHOW ME


How you would check the condition of the Mudguards on this vehicle.

Mudguards

    1. Walkaround
    2. Visual
    3. Kick
    4. Intact
    5. Nothing stuck

SHOW ME


How you would check the windscreen washer level on this vehicle.

Windscreen washer

    1. On Volvo B7 RLE & Scania (single decker) - in front panel
    2. Trident (double decker) - in front panel
    3. Enviro 400 (double decker) - No access
    4. Between minimum and maximum
    5. Operates from inside

SHOW ME


How you would check the wheel nuts are secure on this vehicle.

Wipers

    1. Walkaround
    2. Inspect the Wheel Nuts
    3. Wheel nut indicator tags fitted
    4. Wheel nut indicator tags pointing
    5. IF no wheel tags check for rust behind the Nuts

SHOW ME


How you would check the operation (Horn/Reversing)of audible warning devices.

Audible Warning

    1. Press the Horn (usually the middle of wheel)
    2. Handbrake & Ignition (switch 1 to C)
    3. Select Reverse Gear
    4. Operate the Switch
    5. Listen

Lights

tachograp

Where the Fire Extinguisher is on this Vehicle.

Extinguisher

    1. Find it on the inital safety walk around check
    2. Should be in the Cab area or close by
    3. Powder and CO2 for electric
    4. Foam and water for seats and rubber tyres
    5. Foam for Fuel.

How you check the power assisted steering is working.

Power Steering

    1. Handbrake & Neutral
    2. Master Switch
    3. Turn on Ignition and Engine
    4. Steering wheel moves easily
    5. Wheels move easily

How you would check that the indicators are working.

Indicators

    1. Handbrake and Neutral
    2. Switch Hazard lights on
    3. Walkaround
    4. Working, clean, not damaged
    5. Check the stalk on steering wheel & Dashboard

What Instrument checks you make before and after starting the engine.

Instrument Checks

    1. Handbrake & Neutral
    2. Turn the ignition
    3. Check Dashboard lights on, audible alarms. Then extinguished.
    4. Fuel for the journey
    5. Pressure Guages

How you would clean the windscreen using the washer and wipers.

Wipers

    1.Handbrake and neutral
    2. Ignition on
    3. Pull back the stork
    4. View the effects
    5. Check normal, intermittent and fast.

How you would set the windscreen demister to clear the windows effectively.

Demister

    1. Handbrake and Neutral
    2. Ignition On
    3. Set Air Direction & Speed
    4. Set Air Temperature
    5. Observe effects.

SHOW ME


How you would switch on the rear fog lights.

Fog lights

    1. Handbrake & Neutral
    2. Master Switch & ignition on
    3. Full Lights & pull up for FOG lights
    4. Dashboard and WalKaround
    5. Use when vision 100 metres or less.

SHOW ME


How you would switch your lights from dipped to main.

Dipped lights

    1. Handbrake & Neutral
    2. Master Switch & ignition on
    3. Full Lights & pull back on stalk
    4. Observe bluelight on dashboard
    5. Make sure it is off after use.

The emergency fuel cut off switch on the vehicle.

Fuel cut off

    1. Handbrake & Neutral
    2. Engine off
    3. Open Rear/engine panel
    4. Point to Cut off Switch/handle
    5. Ensure Panel is firmly closed.
